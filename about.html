About Us

Michigan Invasives is a non-profit American organization formed in 2002 by Albert Kooberstein. With goals to educate the public on the dangers of invasive species and lack of biodiversity, Michigan Invasives sets up monthly workshops to demonstrate the harmful effects of dangerous plants, as well as provide tools and knowledge to fight the problem.
